# Solutions Consultant (Atlan)

_Excerpted from "Atlan Solutions Group & Delivery Model | 2026"._

### 
Solutions Consultants (formerly known as TAMs)

Primary Focus:
Strategic implementation leadership and customer technical partnership

Customers need a trusted technical advisor who understands both their business context and Atlan's capabilities to bridge strategy with execution.

Core Responsibilities:

· 
Serve as the primary technical point of contact
for implementation of Success Plan use cases

· 
Translate business problems into technical implementation plans
with clear milestones and deliverables

· 
Advise on technical strategy
and best practices for governance, metadata management, and data operations

· 
Implement solutions
using existing products, certified apps/solutions, and platform capabilities

· 
Collaborate with Solutions Architects
when net-new solution builds are required

· 
Enable customers
to become self-sufficient in operating and extending Atlan

· While CSAs take the lead on most solution builds, it is encouraged for SCs to 
build
where they are able to.

Example engagement:

Customer challenge:
"We have 50+ data sources and no consistent documentation. Our data teams waste hours searching for the right datasets."

Solutions Consultant approach:

1. Assess current state and define target metadata maturity

2. Design phased implementation plan: connectors → classification → glossary → search optimization

3. Guide execution across quarters, adjusting based on adoption metrics

4. Enable customer team to maintain and evolve the solution

What this role does NOT do:

· Focus solely on connector deployment work (owned by Implementation Engineers)

· Own the customer relationship or commercial outcomes (owned by CSMs)
